## Complete SEO Workflow (5 minutes active work)

1. **Research keyword** with OpenClaw
2. **Generate content outline** based on search intent
3. **Write 1500-word blog post** optimized for the keyword
4. **Optimize for SEO** — meta tags, headings, internal links
5. **Add internal links** to relevant pages on your site
6. **Publish to WordPress** directly from Telegram
7. **Create social media posts** promoting the article
8. **Share on Twitter/X** via Bird skill or browser automation

**Result:** From keyword to published, promoted content in minutes.

## Content Creation Pipeline

### Setup
- Drop files (PDFs, notes, transcripts) into a designated folder
- OpenClaw scans the folder daily
- Content is grouped by topic
- Content ideas with titles and hooks are generated

### Daily Automation
- Each morning, OpenClaw sends the **top 3 content ideas**
- You pick one
- OpenClaw creates the full piece of content
- You review and approve
- OpenClaw publishes and promotes

**Result:** Consistent content output with minimal daily effort.

## Lead Management System

### How It Works
1. Leads come into your inbox (email, form submissions, DMs)
2. OpenClaw scans every **30 minutes**
3. Extracts: **name, company, need, timeline, budget**
4. Adds to **Google Sheet** for tracking
5. **Drafts personalized response** based on lead details
6. Sends you **Telegram message** with lead details and draft
7. You approve the response
8. OpenClaw sends it

**Result:** Automated lead qualification and response drafting.
