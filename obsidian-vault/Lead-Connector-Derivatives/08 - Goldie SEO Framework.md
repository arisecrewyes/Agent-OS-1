## Overview

The Goldie SEO Framework is a systematic process for building AI-automated SEO websites. Proven across 6+ case studies with brand new domains (DR 0, 0 backlinks) generating thousands of clicks per month.

**Core Principle:** Combine AI content generation with human quality control and ethical SEO practices.

**The Goldilocks Launch Strategy:** Balance AI efficiency with human quality — AI for speed/scale, humans for editing and fact-checking.

---

## The 5 Phases

### Phase 1: Stake Your Claim (Domain + Niche)
1. Choose a niche (avoid YMYL topics)
2. Use ChatGPT to brainstorm domain names
3. Check availability on Namecheap/GoDaddy/Cloudns
4. Purchase domain ($7-15 typical)
5. For multiple sites: use Easy Blog Networks for hosting

### Phase 2: Mine for Gold (Keyword Research)
1. Enter niche keyword into Ahrefs Keyword Explorer
2. Filter for **Keyword Difficulty ≤ 10**
3. Focus on question-based keywords (how to, what is, why)
4. Remove duplicates and cannibalized keywords
5. Aim for 50-100 keywords minimum
6. Use ChatGPT to cluster keywords
7. **Free alternative:** AnswerThePublic.com → upload image to ChatGPT → extract questions

### Phase 3: Craft Your Gold (Content Creation)
1. Format keywords as article titles (title case)
2. Import into Autoblogging AI (Bolt Generate Mode)
3. Configure: language, length, tone, SERP analysis
4. Generate 50+ article drafts
5. **CRITICAL:** Set to DRAFT mode in WordPress — do NOT publish directly
6. Monetize with ads + email capture

### Phase 4: Refine Your Gold (Quality Control)
The SEO Checklist:
1. Match search intent
2. Demonstrate E-E-A-T
3. Format nicely (headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
4. Add images and videos
5. Short, punchy introduction
6. Internal links to related articles
7. External links to authoritative sources
8. **Fact-check everything** (AI hallucinates)
9. Reduce AI detection score
10. Add unique insights (personal experience, original data)

**Scaling:** Hire VA for editing (15-20 min/article, fixed price)

### Phase 5: Publish, Index & Build Authority
1. Review all 50 articles against checklist
2. Publish all 50 on day one (batch publishing)
3. Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
4. Request indexing for all pages
5. Set up internal linking between articles
6. **Optional:** Cold email link building (quality over quantity)

## Key Principles

1. **Topical Authority > Individual Keywords** — Cover 50+ topics
2. **Quality Control > Raw AI** — Always edit and fact-check
3. **KD ≤ 10 for New Sites** — Build authority with easy wins
4. **50 Articles on Day 1** — Launch with authority
5. **Draft Before Publish** — Never auto-publish raw AI
6. **Content Before Design** — Traffic first, aesthetics later
7. **Ethical AI Use** — Not spam; AI efficiency + human quality
8. **Link Building Multiplies Results** — AI + backlinks = exponential
