## Important Note

GoStackBase does NOT have public funnel share links like GoHighLevel. Templates are inside the platform dashboard. You must recreate GHL funnels using GoStackBase's builder.

**GoStackBase Entry Points:**
- Home: https://gostackbase.com
- Login: https://gostackbase.com/login
- Pricing: https://gostackbase.com/#pricing

## Template Mapping

### 1. Book a Call Funnel
- **GHL Link:** [Book a Call](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=65914bbf139166048fcfe1ba)
- **GoStackBase:** Smart Form → Calendar → Thank You
- **Steps:** Landing Page → Smart Form → Smart Calendar → Confirmation

### 2. ChatGPT Opt-In Funnel
- **GHL Link:** [ChatGPT Opt-In](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=656e6e2df0c86b78a686659f)
- **GoStackBase:** Lead Capture → Email Delivery → Thank You
- **Steps:** Landing Page → Smart Form → Automation (email resource) → Thank You

### 3. Mastermind Funnel
- **GHL Link:** [Mastermind](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=659cb7e834165c150b02f723)
- **GoStackBase:** Application Form → Calendar → Checkout → Welcome
- **Steps:** Multi-step Form → Calendar → Payment → Welcome sequence

### 4. Course Tripwire Funnel
- **GHL Link:** [Course Tripwire](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=659d112e5e5d54211a93ae69)
- **GoStackBase:** Sales Page → Checkout → Upsell → Thank You
- **Steps:** Sales Page → Checkout (Premium+) → Upsell → Delivery

### 5. Free Book Funnel
- **GHL Link:** [Free Book](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=659d112e5e5d54211a93ae69)
- **GoStackBase:** Lead Capture → Email Delivery → Thank You
- **Steps:** Landing Page → Smart Form → Email download link → Thank You
