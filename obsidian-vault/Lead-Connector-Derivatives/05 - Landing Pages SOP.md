# 05 — Landing Pages SOP

> Full SOP for building landing pages, opt-in funnels, and websites with AI.
> Source: Julian Goldie SEO — Skool Post #6
> Parent: [[Lead-Connector-Derivatives/00-index]]

---

## Phase 1: Planning

### Step 1: Define Your Offer
- What are you selling/promoting?
- Who is the target audience?
- What is the desired action? (book a call, opt in, buy, download)
- What's the value proposition?

### Step 2: Choose Your Platform

| Platform | Best For | Price |
|----------|----------|-------|
| GoHighLevel | Full marketing suite | $97/mo |
| GoStackBase | Digital products, client portals | $79/mo |
| BuildwithOS | AI-first | $97/mo |

### Step 3: Choose Your Template
- **Book a Call** — for booking appointments
- **ChatGPT Opt-In** — for lead capture with AI resource
- **Mastermind** — for high-ticket applications
- **Course Tripwire** — for low-cost upsell funnels
- **Free Book** — for lead magnet delivery

## Phase 2: Build the Landing Page

### Step 4: Set Up Page Structure
- Opt-in pages: Form → Thank You/Confirmation
- Sales pages: Sales → Checkout → Upsell → Thank You
- Funnels: Opt-in → Sales → Checkout → Delivery

### Step 5: Connect Your Domain
- Set up root domain or subdomain
- Configure DNS settings
- Enable SSL (usually automatic)

## Phase 3: Generate Content with AI

### Step 6: Generate Headline & Subheadline
Use Claude (recommended) with Prompt 1, then 10 more with Alex Hormozi style (Prompt 2).

### Step 7: Generate Full Page Content
Do it in parts: headline first, then each section individually.

## Phase 4: Set Up Automations

### Step 8-11: Email, CRM, Pipelines
- Set up sending domain and confirmation emails
- Create pipelines and automation triggers
- Connect email marketing platform

## Phase 5: Build Full Website (Auto Blogging)

### Step 12-14: Content + SEO
- Set up autoblogging with AI
- Build website pages (home, about, services, blog, contact)
- Optimize for SEO and submit sitemap

## Phase 6: Test & Launch

### Step 15-16: Test Everything → Launch
- Test forms, emails, links, mobile, checkout
- Publish and monitor conversion rates
- A/B test headlines, CTAs, layouts

## Key Principles

1. **Use templates** — Don't build from scratch
2. **Claude for content** — Best AI for copywriting
3. **Part by part** — Don't rewrite everything at once
4. **Test before launch** — Always verify forms, emails, links
5. **Don't copy competitors** — Personalize everything
6. **More context = Better results** — Give AI detailed information
