## Overview

HighLevel provides 5 pre-built funnel templates that can be added to any account with one click. Each includes pre-designed pages and pre-written copy.

**How to Use:**
1. Click the template link
2. Click "Add funnel" in HighLevel
3. Customize to your business

> ⚠️ **Important:** Do NOT use the same words from the original sales copy/testimonials. Use the template structure and design, but personalize all copy.

---

## Template 1: Book a Call Funnel

**Link:** [Book a Call Funnel](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=65914bbf139166048fcfe1ba)

**Purpose:** Book appointments or calls with potential clients.
**Pages:** Opt-in → Booking → Confirmation
**Best For:** Service businesses, consultants, coaches, agencies

## Template 2: ChatGPT Opt-In Funnel

**Link:** [ChatGPT Opt-In Funnel](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=656e6e2df0c86b78a686659f)

**Purpose:** Capture leads by offering a free AI-related resource.
**Pages:** Opt-in → Delivery/Thank You
**Best For:** Lead generation, building email list, AI/tech audiences

**Original Headline Template:**
```
200+ FREE AI SEO Strategies & VIDEO COURSE -
Steal The EXACT SEO Strategies That 2X'd My Traffic in 11 Days!
```

## Template 3: Mastermind Funnel

**Link:** [Mastermind Funnel](https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=goldie-agency54&funnel_share=659cb7e834165c150b02f723)
