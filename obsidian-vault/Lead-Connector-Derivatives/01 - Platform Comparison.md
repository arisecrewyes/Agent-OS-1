## The 4 Platforms

All derived from the same Lead Connector (LC) core:

```
Lead Connector (Original)
├── GoHighLevel (GHL) — Most popular, agencies, $97/mo
├── LeadConnector (LC) — Original platform, $97/mo
├── GoStackBase — Digital products, client portals, $79/mo
└── BuildwithOS — AI-first, highest affiliate payouts, $97/mo
```

## Key Differentiators

- **GoStackBase:** Client portal is the key differentiator — all clients get their own branded portal
- **BuildwithOS:** Most AI-forward — voice/chat agents, phone calls, MCP support, project ontology
- **GoHighLevel:** Most popular, fullest feature set, biggest community
- **LeadConnector:** The original platform, now separate entity

## Choosing Guide

| Your Need | Best Platform |
|-----------|--------------|
| Agency managing clients | GoHighLevel |
| Digital products + client portal | GoStackBase |
| AI agents + highest affiliate payouts | BuildwithOS |
| Local business simplicity | LeadConnector |
