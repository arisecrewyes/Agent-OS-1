## The Core Problem

Your AI forgets you the second you close the tab. You paste the same context paragraph every day. Exhausting.

## The Solution: Goldie Second Brain Stack

5 layers:
1. **Vault** — Obsidian stores all notes, goals, client folders
2. **Bridge** — Agent reads the vault (tell it once: "notes live here")
3. **Shared Brain** — ALL agents read the SAME vault → one voice, one business
4. **Auto-Notes** — AI writes and organizes notes for you automatically
5. **Loop** — Every note makes the AI sharper; compounds over time

## Key Insight

The "shared brain" trick is what most people miss. Point Claude, Hermes, OpenClaw all at the same vault → they speak about your business in one voice. Like teammates who've for years.

## 30-Day Plan Summary

- **Week 1 (Foundation):** Setup vault, add About/Goals/People, connect agent, test
- **Week 2 (Automate):** Auto-notes, content engine, dashboard, voice training
- **Week 3 (Scale):** Second agent, shared brain test, research, ideation
- **Week 4 (Systematise):** Daily priorities, weekly reviews, automation audit
