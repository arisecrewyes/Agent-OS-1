# Hermes Agent — First 7 Days (Training Period)

> Part of the [[00 - Index|Hermes Agent Full Course]] knowledge base.

## Critical Mindset

**Do NOT treat Hermes like a regular chatbot.** It learns and improves over time. The first 7 days are a training period.

## What Happens During Training

### Day 1-3: Learning You
Hermes builds two key files:
- **memory.md** — Your project context, conventions, preferences
- **user.md** — How you communicate, what you care about

These files get **injected into the system prompt** at the start of every session. This is how Hermes "remembers" you.

### Day 4-7: Applying Knowledge
- Hermes already knows your projects
- Knows where you left off
- Knows your preferences without you repeating yourself
- Can handle complex tasks
- Creates **new skills automatically** after complex tasks (typically 5+ tool calls)

## Key Mindset for the First Week

- **It will not be perfect.** It will make mistakes. That is normal.
- **The point is it gets better every single day.**
- **Give it feedback on every task.** When it messes up, tell it what went wrong and how you want it done.
- It writes this feedback into its **skill.md files** and improves next time.
- Give it **real tasks**, not toy examples.

## By Day 7
- Dramatically better than Day 1
- Understands your workflows
- Has created several custom skills
- Knows your preferences and communication style
- Can handle complex multi-step tasks

## The Compounding Effect

The more you use it, the better it gets. This is a **positive feedback loop**:
1. You give it a task
2. It does the task (imperfectly at first)
3. You give feedback
4. It updates its skills
5. Next time, it does the task better
6. Repeat forever
