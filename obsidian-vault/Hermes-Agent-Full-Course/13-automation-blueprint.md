> Part of the [[00 - Index|Hermes Agent Full Course]] knowledge base.

## The 3-Step Automation Blueprint

Every automation follows this same process:

### Step 1: Where do you spend your time?
Identify the repetitive task. Be specific about what eats your time.

### Step 2: What is the one thing you can automate this week?
Pick **one specific task**. Don't try to automate everything at once.

### Step 3: Ask Hermes how to automate it.
Say: *"Based on what you know about me, what could you help me automate day-to-day specifically for me?"*

Then:
1. **Define the trigger** — when should it run?
2. **Give it the data sources** — APIs, credentials, URLs
3. **Test it once** — make sure it works manually
4. **Schedule it** — tell Hermes the frequency

## Proven Use Cases

### Social Media
- ✅ Posting to **X/Twitter** with AI-generated images and trending content
- ✅ Posting to **TikTok** with captions and hashtags
- ✅ Posting to **Instagram** with captions and hashtags

### Research & Monitoring
- ✅ Researching **Reddit** for newest trending topics in your niche
- ✅ **Competitor research** with breakdown of trending topics, what is working, who posted, and their analytics
- ✅ **Daily AI news scanning** and summarization
- ✅ **Web browsing and research** with Firecrawl and Browser Base

### Analytics
- ✅ Daily breakdown of **social media analytics** with screenshots
- ✅ Weekly **X analytics** — followers, engagement, top tweets

### Content Pipeline
- ✅ Research trending topic → Draft post → Generate image → Post to platform → Send confirmation

### Lead Management
- ✅ Scan inbox → Extract lead details → Add to sheet → Draft response → Send for approval

## Key Principles

1. **The more you use it, the better it gets.** This is a compounding effect.
2. **Always give feedback on every task.** This is how it improves.
3. **String multiple skills together.** Skill 1 (research) + Skill 2 (posting) + Skill 3 (image gen) = full automated pipeline.
4. **Create your own skills** rather than only relying on community ones. Custom skills are tailored exactly to your workflow.
5. **Back up your skill.md files weekly.**
6. **Be prepared for the first 7 days.** It will not be perfect but it gets dramatically better by day 7.
7. **If posting to social media, always ask for a draft first** and approve before posting. Do this at least 10 times before letting it post autonomously.
