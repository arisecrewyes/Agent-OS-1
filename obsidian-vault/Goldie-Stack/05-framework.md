## The Problem: The Blank Slate Problem

Most people use AI agents the old way:
1. Open the tool
2. Type a long paragraph explaining who you are
3. Get an answer
4. Close it
5. Tomorrow, do it all again

Your AI is powerful, but it's like a genius with amnesia. Every conversation = starting from zero.

---

## The Solution: Four Layers

### 🔵 Layer 1: CAPTURE
OMI records everything you say, do, and think. Your words. Your screen. Your actions. All captured silently. No effort required.

### 🟣 Layer 2: ORGANISE
OMI processes raw recordings into clean, structured memory notes. Goals. Preferences. Schedules. Clients. Projects. Habits. Organised into a memory map that grows smarter every day.

### 🟢 Layer 3: STORE
Obsidian holds your memory vault. Safe. Private. Yours. No cloud dependency. No subscription. A living knowledge base about you.

### 🟡 Layer 4: DEPLOY
Your AI agents read the vault and begin every conversation already knowing you. Hermes. OpenClaw. Claude Code. Any local AI tool. One file path. Full context. Infinite leverage.
