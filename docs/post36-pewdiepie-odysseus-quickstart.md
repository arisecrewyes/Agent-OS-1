## What Is Odysseus?

A **free, open-source AI workspace** that runs on **your own computer** — launched by PewDiePie on May 31st 2026. It hit 30,000 GitHub stars in two days.

**Core idea:** Instead of renting AI from a big company (ChatGPT, Claude), you own the app and the data lives on your machine. No monthly fees.

**Components inside one app:**
- **Chat** — everyday AI helper (like ChatGPT but private)
- **Agent (Operator)** — autonomous worker that plans and executes tasks
- **Research** — deep web research engine
- **Notes, Calendar, Email** — built-in productivity tools
- **Memory** — remembers your documents, chats, and preferences over time

## The Odysseus Operator System (Framework)

A 4-layer framework for thinking about the tool:

| Layer | Name | What It Does |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | 🧠 **The Brain** | The AI model (local or cloud via OpenRouter) |
| 2 | 💬 **The Chatter** | Everyday Q&A and writing helper |
| 3 | 🤖 **The Operator** | Autonomous agent that does tasks for you |
| 4 | 📚 **The Memory** | Remembers your docs, notes, and past chats |

## 100+ Copy-Paste Prompts

### Setup & Brain
- "Here's a GitHub project: [link]. Walk me through installing it step by step like I've never used a command line."
- "What's the difference between a local brain and a cloud brain? Explain it like I'm 10."
- "Which tools should a beginner turn OFF for safety? Explain why."

### Daily Chatter
- "Explain [topic] in simple terms with one real-life example."
- "Give me 3 fresh angles to talk about [topic]."
- "Turn this messy idea into 3 clear sentences: [idea]."

### Operator Tasks
- "Research the 3 newest free AI tools this week and summarize them with emojis."
- "Find the top 5 questions people ask about [topic]."
- "Make me a short morning briefing of today's [industry] news."

### Scheduled Tasks
- "Every morning at 6am, find the newest AI tool launches and summarize them."
- "Each morning, summarize my unread emails into a simple list."
- "Weekly, research what's new in [topic] and write a recap."

### Memory & Documents
- "Read my documents and tell me the 3 main themes."
- "Summarize this document in 5 simple sentences: [file]."
- "Turn my messy notes into a clean outline."

### Visual Builds (CodePen)
- "Write code for a glowing neon dashboard with big bold numbers on a dark background."
- "Make a colorful page with floating bubbles that pop when clicked."
- "Build a retro arcade score screen with glowing letters."

### Content Creation
- "Turn this frustration into a punchy social post: [frustration]."
- "Write 5 short posts from this one idea: [idea]."
- "Write a short script for a 60-second video about [topic]."

### Research Engine
- "Do deep research on the history of [topic] in simple terms."
- "Find the most common beginner mistakes in [field]."
- "Research what people wish they knew before starting [activity]."

### Systematise & Delegate
- "Turn this task I do often into a simple repeatable checklist."
- "What parts of this task could a worker do without me?"
- "List the 3 tasks I should automate first and why."

### Mindset & Momentum
- "Give me one tiny step to start [scary task] today."
- "Turn my excuse into an action step: [excuse]."
- "Help me write down 3 wins from today, no matter how small."
