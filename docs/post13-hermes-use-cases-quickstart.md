## The 24/7 Business Machine™ Framework

A five-layer system for building a fully automated business:

### 🔵 Layer 1: THE BRAIN
Your AI agent builds a permanent memory of your business, clients, goals, and voice. It never forgets and gets smarter every day.

### 🟣 Layer 2: THE WORKERS
Hermes Agent spawns sub-agents and assigns tasks in parallel — one researches, one writes, one builds, one reports back. All simultaneously.

### 🟢 Layer 3: THE SYSTEMS
SOPs, workflows, and skills are saved permanently. First time you solve a problem, Hermes saves the solution as a skill. Next time, it already knows what to do.

### 🟡 Layer 4: THE PIPELINE
Automated cron jobs, scheduled tasks, and proactive messages run 24/7:
- Morning briefings delivered to your phone
- Weekly reports written and sent automatically
- Content calendars planned and scheduled
- Client follow-ups done before you wake up

### 🔴 Layer 5: THE GROWTH LOOP
The system monitors its own performance, improves its own skills, and continuously compounds its capabilities. Your AI gets better every single day without you doing anything.
