## Overview

The Goldie SEO Framework is a systematic process for building AI-automated SEO websites that generate organic traffic from Google. It has been proven across multiple case studies with brand new domains (DR 0, 0 backlinks) generating thousands of clicks per month.

**Core Principle:** Combine AI content generation with human quality control and ethical SEO practices to build topical authority faster than traditional methods.

**The Goldilocks Launch Strategy:** Balance AI efficiency with human quality — use AI for speed and scale, use humans for editing, fact-checking, and ensuring content quality.

## The 5 Phases

### Phase 1: Stake Your Claim (Domain + Niche Selection)

**Goal:** Find a profitable niche and secure the right domain.

**Steps:**
1. Choose a niche you want to rank in (avoid YMYL topics — "Your Money or Your Life")
2. Use ChatGPT to brainstorm domain name ideas:
   ```
   Give me domain names for a [YOUR NICHE] website
   ```
3. Check domain availability on Namecheap, GoDaddy, or Cloudns
4. Purchase the domain ($7-15 typical for available domains)
5. For multiple sites: use Easy Blog Networks for hosting to hide footprint across IPs

**Key Considerations:**
- Brand new domains work (all case studies used new domains, not expired)
- Avoid niches where Google demands high E-E-A-T (health, finance, legal)
- Service businesses, info products, ads, affiliate — all work with this method

---

### Phase 2: Mine for Gold (Keyword Research)

**Goal:** Find 50-100 low-competition, question-based keywords to build topical authority.

**Tools:**
- **Ahrefs** (paid) — Keyword Explorer with keyword difficulty filter
- **Answer The Public** (free) — Find all questions people ask about a topic
- **ChatGPT** — Brainstorm keyword ideas and extract questions from Answer The Public images

**Steps:**
1. Enter your main niche keyword into Ahrefs Keyword Explorer
2. Filter for **Keyword Difficulty (KD) 10 or less**
3. Focus on **question-based keywords** (how to, what is, why does, etc.)
4. Remove duplicates and cannibalized keywords (same question = one page)
5. Aim for 50-100 keywords minimum
6. Use ChatGPT to cluster and organize keywords:
   ```
   Cluster these [NICH] keywords into logical groups: [PASTE KEYWORDS]
   ```

**Why KD 10 or Less?**
- New domains have no authority/trust from Google
- Low competition keywords are easier to rank for initially
- AI is powerful for generating Q&A content (matches Google's AI Overviews format)
- Build topical authority with volume, not difficulty

**Free Alternative (Answer The Public):**
1. Go to AnswerThePublic.com
2. Enter your niche keyword
3. Select country and language
4. Hit search
5. Save the visualization image
6. Upload to ChatGPT and ask: "Extract all the questions from this image"

---

### Phase 3: Craft Your Gold (Content Creation with AI)

**Goal:** Generate 50+ article drafts using AI autoblogging tools.

**Primary Tool:** Autoblogging AI (https://autobloggingai.com)
- Bolt Generate Mode: Generate 50 article drafts at once
- Quick Mode: Generate individual articles
- Supports multiple languages
- Auto-post to WordPress (use with caution — always draft first)

**Steps:**
1. Take your keyword list from Phase 2
2. Format keywords as article titles (title case)
3. Import into Autoblogging AI (manual entry or CSV)
4. Configure settings:
   - Language: English
   - Length: Medium
   - Tone: Match your industry (B2C = conversational, B2B = professional)
   - SERP Analysis: Enable (reverse-engineer competitors)
   - First person perspective recommended
5. Generate content in bulk (50+ articles)
6. **CRITICAL:** Set to DRAFT mode in WordPress — do NOT publish directly

**Why 50 Articles on Day 1?**
- Builds immediate topical authority
- One article on a new domain = no trust = no rankings
- 50 articles = signals expertise to Google
- All three case studies launched with 50+ articles on day one

**Monetization:**
- Display ads (Google AdSense, Mediavine, etc.)
- Email capture within content (remarketing)
- Affiliate links where relevant

---

### Phase 4: Refine Your Gold (Quality Control + SEO Checklist)

**Goal:** Transform raw AI content into high-quality, ranking-worthy articles.

**The SEO Checklist (from Goldie SEO Framework):**
1. **Match search intent** — Does the content answer what the searcher wants?
2. **Demonstrate E-E-A-T** — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
3. **Format nicely** — Headers, bullet points, short paragraphs, visual hierarchy
4. **Add images and videos** — Visual content improves engagement and rankings
5. **Short, punchy introduction** — Get to the point immediately
6. **Internal links** — Link to related articles on your site
7. **External links** — Link to authoritative sources
8. **Fact-check** — AI hallucinates; verify all claims and statistics
9. **Reduce AI detection score** — Make content sound natural and human
10. **Add unique insights** — Personal experience, original data, expert quotes

**Scaling Quality Control:**
- Hire a virtual assistant for editing (pay per article, fixed price)
- Budget 15-20 minutes per article for editing
- Use the checklist as a step-by-step guide for VAs

**Anti-Spam Guarantee (Golden Shield):**
- Never publish raw AI content
- Always edit and fact-check
- Add human expertise and unique perspectives
- Focus on helping the reader, not just ranking

---

### Phase 5: Publish, Index & Build Authority

**Goal:** Get content indexed and start building domain authority.

**Publishing Steps:**
1. Review all 50 articles against the SEO checklist
2. Publish all 50 articles on day one (batch publishing)
3. Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
4. Request indexing for all pages
5. Set up proper internal linking between articles

**Link Building (Optional but Powerful):**
- Cold email outreach to relevant sites
- Personalized emails mentioning specific content on their site
- Offer value (guest posts, resource pages, collaborations)
- Quality over quantity — natural, relevant links only
- Avoid spam: no profile links, no forum signatures, no comment spam
- Use Ahrefs to find sites ranking for relevant keywords
- Reference: Free Link Building Mastery Book (Julian Goldie)
- Reference: 42.SEO Link Building Checklist

## Key Principles

1. **Topical Authority > Individual Keywords** — Cover 50+ topics to signal expertise
2. **Quality Control > Raw AI** — Always edit, fact-check, and add human value
3. **KD 10 or Less for New Sites** — Build authority with easy wins first
3. **50 Articles on Day 1** — Launch with authority, not one article at a time
4. **Draft Before Publish** — Never auto-publish raw AI content
5. **Content Before Design** — Traffic first, aesthetics later
6. **Ethical AI Use** — Not spam; combine AI efficiency with human quality
7. **Link Building Multiplies Results** — AI content + backlinks = exponential growth
