## Essential Prompts

### Prompt 1: Rewrite Headline + Subheadline
```
Rewrite this headline and subheadline:
200+ FREE AI SEO Strategies & VIDEO COURSE -
Steal The EXACT SEO Strategies That 2X'd My Traffic in 11 Days!
But personalize this for my offer: INSERT YOUR OFFER HERE
```

**What it does:** Takes the proven HighLevel template headline and rewrites it for your specific offer while maintaining the proven structure (number + benefit + proof).

**Expected output:** A personalized headline + subheadline that follows the same conversion formula.

---

### Prompt 2: Alex Hormozi Style Headlines
```
OK now give me 10 more, Alex Hormozi style
```

**What it does:** Generates 10 additional headline variations in Alex Hormozi's style (direct, benefit-driven, pattern interrupt, specific numbers).

**Expected output:** 10 headline options like:
1. "The [Adjective] [Offer] That [Specific Result] in [Timeframe]"
2. "How [Person/Type] [Achieved Result] Without [Common Pain]"
3. "[Number] [Audience] Use This [Thing] to [Result]"

---

### Prompt 3: Rewrite Body Section
```
Now rewrite this section: INSERTHERE
```

**What it does:** Rewrites a specific section of your landing page copy while maintaining the overall structure.

**Usage:** Copy/paste any section (testimonials, features, benefits, guarantee, etc.) and get a rewritten version personalized for your offer.

## Supporting Prompts

### Opt-In Page Prompt
```
Write an opt-in page for [OFFER]. Include:
- A compelling headline that creates curiosity
- 3-5 bullet points of what they'll get
- A single CTA button text
- Keep it under 200 words
```

### Sales Page Prompt
```
Write a sales page for [OFFER] targeting [AUDIENCE]. Include:
- Attention-grabbing headline
- Problem agitation
- Solution introduction
- Benefits (not features)
- Social proof section
- Guarantee
- CTA with urgency
- Price: [PRICE]
```

### Email Sequence Prompt
```
Write a 5-email welcome sequence for people who signed up for [OFFER].
Email 1: Deliver the resource + introduce yourself
Email 2: Share your story / why you created this
Email 3: Provide additional value / quick win
Email 4: Address common objections
Email 5: Soft pitch for [MAIN OFFER]
```

### Blog Post Prompt
```
Write a blog post about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE].
Target keyword: [KEYWORD]
Word count: 1500-2000 words
Include: H2/H3 headers, intro, body, conclusion, CTA
Tone: Conversational, authoritative, helpful
```

### Landing Page Optimization Prompt
```
Review this landing page copy and suggest improvements:
[PASTE COPY HERE]
Focus on: headline clarity, benefit focus, CTA strength, social proof, urgency
```

## Recommended AI Tools

| Tool | Best For | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| **Claude** | All copywriting tasks | Best for marketing copy, understands nuance |
| **ChatGPT** | Brainstorming, ideation | Good for generating many variations |
| **Claude Code** | Technical implementation | Can build actual landing pages |
| **Hermes Agent** | Automation | Can trigger and manage entire funnels |

> **Julian's recommendation:** Always use Claude for writing content. Hermes Agent for managing and automating the funnel.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Start with Prompt 1** — Get your headline right first (most important element)
2. **Use Prompt 2** — Get 10 more variations to choose from
3. **Use Prompt 3** — Rewrite each section of your page one at a time
4. **Review and edit** — Always add your personal touch
5. **Test** — A/B test different headline variations
