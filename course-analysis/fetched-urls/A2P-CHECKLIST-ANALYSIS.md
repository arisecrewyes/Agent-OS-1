## THE ROOT DOMAIN TRAP

One of the most common A2P rejection reasons: **the root domain, not the subdomain.**

Reviewers delete the subdomain part and check the main site (e.g., `app.client.com` → they check `client.com`).

**Will be REJECTED if root domain is:**
- **Broken:** 404 error or parking page
- **Incomplete:** "Coming Soon" or "Under Construction"
- **Non-Compliant:** Missing SMS checkbox/legal text on contact form
- **Mismatched:** Different brand name than application

### Two Workarounds

**Option 1: The "Redirect" Trick (Free)**
- 301 redirect root domain → compliant funnel subdomain
- When reviewer checks root, they land on compliant page

**Option 2: The "Clean Slate" Method (Safest — ~$12)**
- Buy brand new domain (e.g., `try-clientvip.com`)
- Put funnel on root homepage
- No "other" website for reviewer to find

## BRAND REGISTRATION

### ⚠️ New LLCs — Wait 15 Days
- Third-party databases need 15 days to update after LLC formation
- Apply too early = automatic rejection
- **Solution:** Register as "Sole Proprietor" first (lower daily limits but sufficient to start)
- After 4 weeks, resubmit as Company

### LLC Brand Submission Rules (Character-Perfect)

1. **Legal Business Name** — Must match IRS/SS-4/Tax docs CHARACTER FOR CHARACTER
   - ✅ Right: `Digital Warriors, LLC`
   - ❌ Wrong: `Digital Warriors LLC` (missing comma)
   - ❌ Wrong: `Digital Warriors` (missing suffix)

2. **EIN / Tax ID** — Single wrong digit = immediate rejection

3. **Business Address** — Must match EIN registration address
   - If moved but didn't update IRS → use old address still on file

### Sole Proprietor Submission Rules

1. **Legal Business Name = Full Legal Name** (unless DBA filed)
   - ✅ Right: `John Doe`
   - ❌ Wrong: `Best Marketing Agency`

2. **Mobile Number** (Critical)
   - Must be personal mobile (AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon)
   - NO VoIP (Google Voice, Skype, business lines)
   - OTP verification required during registration

3. **Physical Address** — Must be valid location, not P.O. Box if possible

## CAMPAIGN REGISTRATION

### Use Case Description Template:
> This messaging campaign is used by [LEGAL COMPANY NAME] to send text messages to individuals who have voluntarily opted in to receive updates, service information, scheduling communications, and relevant announcements related to [LEGAL COMPANY NAME]'s offerings.

### Sample Message #1:
> Hello, this is a team member from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME] reaching out regarding your recent inquiry. Let us know if you'd like to continue the conversation or have any questions we can help with. Reply STOP to opt out of future text messages.

### Sample Message #2:
> Hi from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME]. This message is to confirm your upcoming scheduled session. If you need assistance or would like to make changes, you may reply directly. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS notifications.

Users may also opt in by texting START to the business phone number listed on the website.

### Opt-in Message:
> You're subscribed! You have successfully opted in to receive SMS messages from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME]. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

### Opt-in Form/Image URL: https://yourwebsite.com/home

## UNIVERSAL SOP IMPLICATIONS

This A2P checklist is **US-specific (10DLC)**. For the Universal SOP:

1. **The compliance pattern is universal:**
   - Legal entity verification
   - Compliant website with opt-in mechanism
   - Sample messages with opt-out language
   - Carrier registration and approval

2. **Per-country adaptation needed:**
   - US: A2P/10DLC (documented here)
   - EU: GDPR consent requirements (stricter opt-in)
   - UK: PECR compliance
   - Canada: CASL compliance
   - Australia: Spam Act compliance

3. **LC platform differences:**
   - GHL: Built-in Trust Center for A2P
   - StackBase: Same Trust Center (shared infrastructure)
   - BuildwithOS: Same Trust Center (shared infrastructure)
   - Other LC derivatives: Same API, same trust system

4. **The A2P Wizard (a2pwizard.com) generates compliant websites**
   - Provides templates for: website, privacy policy, T&C, consent checkboxes
   - Generates sample messages automatically
   - The wizard itself could be adapted for any compliance regime
