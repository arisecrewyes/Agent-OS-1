WhatsApp Business Integration Guide
Connect your WhatsApp Business account to LeadConnector. Choose the method that fits your situation.
Choose Your Method
1
Connect Your Existing WhatsApp Business App
Keep using your existing app alongside the platform (Coexistence)
2
Connect a New WhatsApp Business Number
Starting fresh with a brand-new number
3
Migrate from an Existing BSP
Switch from another Business Solution Provider
Method 1
Connect Your Existing WhatsApp Business App
Unsupported Countries
The Coexistence feature is not available for phone numbers from
Nigeria
or
South Africa
.
1
Start the Signup Process
Navigate to
Settings > WhatsApp
and select
Connect your WhatsApp
2
Continue with Signup
Follow the on-screen instructions to begin the registration process.
3
Connect to LeadConnector
Click
Continue
to proceed with the integration.
4
Select WhatsApp Business App
Click
Connect a WhatsApp Business App
, then tap
Next
.
5
Enter Your Phone Number
Add your WhatsApp Business phone number and tap
Next
.
6
Verify Your Account Details
Review your WhatsApp Business Account details and make any corrections before proceeding.
7
Import Contacts & Chat History
Import contacts and up to six months of chat history from the WhatsApp Business App into LeadConnector.
Update your WhatsApp Business App to the
latest version
before this step.
Look for a message from the official Facebook Business account and
scan the QR code
when prompted.
8
Connect to the Business Platform
Scan the QR code to complete the connection. You can continue using your WhatsApp Business App alongside the Business Platform.
9
Share Chat History & Contacts
Your contacts and conversations will be securely synced with LeadConnector.
10
Setup Complete
Your WhatsApp Business App is now successfully integrated with LeadConnector.
11
Review and Confirm
Check all entered details carefully, then click
Confirm
.
✓
Integration Complete
Click
Finish
. Your WhatsApp Business App is fully integrated with LeadConnector.
NOTE
1. Sending template messages is not supported via the WhatsApp Business App — they can only be sent through the CRM.
2. You will manage your WhatsApp profile from the WhatsApp Business App, not the CRM.
3. You can only have a maximum of one phone number under Coexistence.
Method 2
Connect a New WhatsApp Business Number
1
Start the Signup Process
Navigate to
Settings > WhatsApp
and click
Create a new WhatsApp business account.
2
Continue with Facebook
Click
Continue with Facebook
and log in using the Facebook account associated with your business.
3
Begin Setup
Tap
Continue
to initiate the integration process.
4
Enter Business Details
Fill in all required business information, select
Create WhatsApp Business Account
, then click
Next
.
5
Add Your Phone Number
Enter the phone number you want to integrate. Make sure it's active and accessible for verification.
6
Verify via OTP
Enter the one-time password received by SMS or voice call, then click
Next
.
7
Review and Confirm
Check all entered details carefully, then click
Confirm
.
✓
Integration Complete
Your WhatsApp Business Account is now successfully integrated and ready to use.
Method 3
Migrate from an Existing BSP
1
Start the Signup Process
Navigate to
Settings > WhatsApp
and click
Migrating from an existing BSP (Business Solution Provider)
.
2
Begin Setup
Tap
Continue
to initiate the migration process.
3
Select Business Assets
Choose the business assets you'd like to migrate. Make sure you select the correct WhatsApp Business Account and associated assets.
4
Enter Business Details
Fill in all required business information accurately, then click
Next
.
5
Add Your Phone Number
Enter the phone number you wish to migrate. Ensure it's active and accessible for verification, then click
Next
.
6
Verify Your Phone Number
Click
Next
to receive your verification code and proceed with number verification.
7
Enter the OTP
Enter the one-time password received by SMS or voice call, then click
Next
.
8
Review and Confirm
Check all entered details carefully, then click
Confirm
to complete the migration.
✓
Migration Complete
Your WhatsApp Account has been successfully migrated and is ready to use.
